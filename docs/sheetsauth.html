Google Sheets Authentication

Welcome and thank you again for purchasing Google Sheets extension!

Now that you successfully authenticated your Google account, you can proceed with retrieving your refresh token.

Please provide your key you retrieved on the Itch.io purchase page:

If you did not retrieve your key from the Itch.io purchase page, you can do so by clicking on the purchase link that was sent to your email.

If you have any issues retrieving your key, please join our Discord server and ask for assistance.


  1. In your Receiver, go to the Google Sheets premade deck which was created when you installed the extension. Make sure your Transmitter is running and connected.
  2. Right click on the red INIT button - edit commands.
  3. Scroll down to Send to Extension command and paste your refresh token into the refreshToken field and your key (retrieved from Itch) into the userKey field (if you haven't done so already).
  4. Press Done twice and follow the rest of the setup instructions.
